About Us

For nearly 50 years, Andrea’s Salumeria has been serving fresh mozzarella, handcrafted sandwiches, and classic Italian specialties in the heart of Jersey City. What started as a neighborhood shop has become a destination for anyone who loves honest, old-world Italian food.

From the Counter & Kitchen

Behind every sandwich and platter is a simple approach: quality ingredients, time‑honored methods, and a commitment to feeding people the way family is fed. Fresh mozzarella is pulled daily, imported and domestic meats and cheeses are sliced to order, and prepared dishes are made in small batches for maximum flavor.
