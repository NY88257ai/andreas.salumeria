Our Story Since 1975

Andrea's Salumeria has been part of the neighborhood for nearly fifty years — a family-run Italian deli where regulars are greeted by name and newcomers feel like they’ve been coming in for years.

From fresh mozzarella pulled daily to imported Italian groceries, cured meats, and classic sandwiches, everything is prepared with old-world care and pride.

Whether you're grabbing a hero for lunch or feeding a packed table for the holidays, Andrea’s is here for your everyday meals and your biggest celebrations.
