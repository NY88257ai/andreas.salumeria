Nearly 50 Years of Italian Tradition in Jersey City

About Us

Since 1975, Andrea’s Salumeria has been a small neighborhood deli with a big heart. Tucked into Jersey City’s Heights, it’s the kind of place where people come for a sandwich and end up staying for conversation, stories, and a taste of the old country. Recipes, techniques, and traditions have been passed down and refined over decades, but the goal has always stayed the same: feed people the way family is fed.

From the Counter & Kitchen

Every day, fresh mozzarella is pulled by hand, meats and cheeses are sliced to order, and classic Italian specialties are prepared in small batches. There’s nothing rushed or factory-made here— just good ingredients, time, and care. Whether it’s a stacked Italian hero, a simple prosciutto and fresh mozz sandwich, or a tray of food for the whole family, everything that leaves the counter is meant to feel homemade, honest, and deeply satisfying.
