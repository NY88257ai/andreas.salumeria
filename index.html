Our story

Family‑owned & operated • Since 1975

Welcome to Andrea's Salumeria, a cherished family‑owned and operated business proudly serving our community since 1975. For nearly five decades, we have been dedicated to providing authentic Italian deli meats, cheeses, and specialties, upholding a tradition of quality and passion passed down through generations.

From our homemade mozzarella and famous sandwiches to imported Italian groceries and catering, every product reflects our commitment to genuine flavor and warm hospitality. Whether you’re grabbing a quick bite, picking up ingredients for Sunday dinner, or planning a celebration, Andrea’s Salumeria is here to share our family’s love for exceptional food with you.

What makes Andrea’s special

  • Nearly 50 years serving our neighborhood
  • Homemade mozzarella crafted in‑house
  • Classic Italian sandwiches and daily specials
  • Fine deli meats, cheeses, and imported groceries
  • Catering available for gatherings and events
Step inside and experience the feel of a traditional Italian salumeria — no shortcuts, just real food.
